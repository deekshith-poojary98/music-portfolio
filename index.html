About Deekshith

Deekshith Poojary is a passionate singer-songwriter and producer who creates music across multiple genres. With a diverse musical palette, Deekshith has developed a unique sound that blends Hindi acoustic, EDM, trap, and indie pop elements.

Born with a deep love for music, Deekshith discovered his passion for creating melodies at an early age. After learning to play guitar and piano, he began writing his own songs, drawing inspiration from life experiences, emotions, and the human condition.

His music spans from emotional Hindi acoustic tracks like "Raaste" to high-energy festival EDM like "Light It Up Tonight", showcasing his versatility as an artist.
